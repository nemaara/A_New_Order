BACKSTORY, A NEW ORDER (put together by Sigil)

627 YW - Gweddry and Dacyn kill Mal-Ravanal (see Eastern Invasion)
673 YW - Konrad II dies, bringing the Garardine dynasty to an end. Second Wesnothian Civil War begins.
(858 YW - Son of the Black Eye ends.)

915 YW - The Akladians break the yoke of Grekulak
918 YW - The Akladian army arrives on the banks of the river at Wesnoth's eastern edge. The second Wesnothian Civil War has left Wesnoth as little more than scattered fiefdoms.
919 YW - The Akladians, led by the first Hagarthen, crush the disparate Wesnothian forces.
923 YW - Weldyn finally falls and is burned to the ground.

926 YW - Lorin of Gallorae is born.
928 YW - Vattin is established.
931 YW - The first Hagarthen divides the Akladian kingdom among his sons, creating Okladia, Vakladia, Easkladia, and Guilcorta.
932 YW - Gawen Hagarthen's father takes a Wesnothian bride from one of the few
noble houses left when Weldyn burned. This causes an uprising, which he
immediately quells. (comment from A.D.D. ...hmm...)
934 YW - Gawen Hagarthen is born.
939 YW - Gawen Hagarthen's mother is poisoned.
940 YW - Gawen Hagarthen's mother dies.
942 YW - Gawen Hagarthen's father marries Lorin of Gallorae.
958 YW - Gawen Hagarthen's father is killed trying to quell another rebellion. The Events of A New Order begin. (Lorin is 32; Gawen is 24).
958 YW - Gawen betrayed at the Battle of Barnon. Soon after, Gaumhaldric begins gathering notoriety with military victories among the Wesnothians. Lorin of Gallorae seizes Haeltin.
959 YW - Gaumhaldric (Gawen) re-unites with Lorin and gains information concerning Grekulak.
959 YW - Spring - Gawen's band has a departing feast in Freetown.
959 YW - Summer - Gawen's band travels through Okladia and visits the oracle.
959 YW - Fall - As Gawen returns to the Oracle with Deorien, Bor Cryne and his men sack Freetown.
959 YW - Winter - The Battle of Orannon. Lorin begins to go into labor on the battlefield.
960 YW - early Winter - Lorin is blinded and exiled. The events of A New Order come to an end.
